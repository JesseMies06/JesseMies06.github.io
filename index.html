My Projects


Unity 3D - Cubert

Play Right Now!

Cubert is a "retro styled" topdown endless runner game that I have been working on with Unity. In the game you play as Cubert the Purple Cube and keep jumping forward, avoiding obstacles and hazards while the ground crumbles behind you. There is also a skin system in the game. Collect coins from the ground and use them to buy skins with custom sound effects.

Click the button below to play Cubert free straight in your browser! No download needed!




VR Game - Prison Breakout VR

[Made during vocational school]

Our VR game is a jailbreak escape room. The aim of the game is to complete puzzles and escape the prison in less than 15 minutes. The puzzles have the right amount of difficulty and variety to make the game suitable for everyone.

We made this game in a three person group. I was in charge of designing and doing almost the entire map, and the win/lose scenes. The game was presented in the Vectorama event in Oulu, where people could come and try the game out.

Gun Guy Zombies

[Was in the making during vocational school]

Gun Guy Zombies is a 3D horror first person shooter made with Unity. The player has to go through a forest full of zombies while following a path to the end. We were making this game as a three person group. I was in charge of designing and doing the entire map and some small details.


Gun Guy RPG 

[Was in the making during vocational school]

Gun Guy RPG is an top down 2D rpg style game made with Unity, where the player goes trough the island, fighting zombies and enemies in a turn based combat style. The player can find money and spend it in stores to buy better equipment.


PaintBall!

[Was in the making during vocational school]

PaintBall! is a 5 man made multiplayer fps game made with Unreal Engine, where the players fight eachother with paintballguns in different gamemodes and team sizes. We had a preview alpha version of the game in the Vectorama event.
